## FFmpeg
License: LGPL 2.1+
Website: https://ffmpeg.org/
Smol bundles a build of FFmpeg from gyan.dev (release-essentials, LGPL build) as an
external sidecar process for video and audio compression. Smol does not statically link
FFmpeg. Source code: https://ffmpeg.org/download.html

## Ghostscript
License: AGPL-3.0
Website: https://www.ghostscript.com/

link against Ghostscript at compile time. Users who receive Smol receive access to
Ghostscript's source code under AGPL-3.0 from:
https://www.ghostscript.com/releases/gsdnld.html
